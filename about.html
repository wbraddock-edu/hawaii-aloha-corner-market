Our Story

A family business rooted in community, aloha spirit, and Molokai pride since 1998.

Est. 1998

Born from Aloha

In 1998, the Kahananui family opened a small corner store on Ala Malama Avenue with a simple mission: make fresh, affordable food accessible to every family on Molokai. What started as a tiny storefront with a few produce crates has grown into the island's most beloved neighborhood market.

For over 26 years, we've been more than a grocery store. We're a gathering place, a community hub, and a way for local farmers and producers to get their products into the hands of Molokai families. We carry over 200 locally grown or made products — because supporting our neighbors is the aloha way.

Today, the second generation of the Kahananui family continues the tradition, keeping prices fair, sourcing local whenever possible, and treating every customer like ohana.
