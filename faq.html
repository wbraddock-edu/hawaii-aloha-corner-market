Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping and ordering at Aloha Corner Market.

What are your store hours?+
We're open Monday through Thursday 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Friday and Saturday 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're open 365 days a year, including most holidays. Hours may vary on major holidays — call ahead if you're unsure.
Do you carry fresh local Molokai produce?+
Yes! Local produce is the heart of what we do. We carry Molokai sweet potatoes year-round, and rotate seasonal fruits and vegetables including taro, breadfruit, mango, papaya, banana, avocado, and more. Our produce deliveries arrive Tuesday and Friday mornings for the freshest selection.
How do I place a catering or bulk order?+
You can request an order through our online Order Request form on this website, or simply call us at (808) 555-0116. For catering orders, we recommend placing your request at least 48–72 hours in advance. For large events (over 100 guests), please give us at least a week's notice.
What kinds of catering do you offer?+
We offer full catering packages for luaus, graduations, family reunions, birthday parties, corporate events, and community gatherings. Our packages include plate lunch setups, Hawaiian party trays, fresh fruit platters, poke, haupia, and more. Pricing starts at approximately $12 per person depending on selections.
Do you deliver on Molokai?+
Yes, we offer delivery within Kaunakakai and surrounding areas for orders over a minimum amount. Delivery is available for catering orders and large bulk purchases. Delivery fees apply depending on distance. Call us at (808) 555-0116 to arrange.
Can I set up a wholesale account for my restaurant or school?+
Absolutely. We offer wholesale pricing and account management for restaurants, cafes, schools, churches, and nonprofit organizations. Contact us to set up an account — we'll discuss your needs, set up a pricing structure, and arrange a regular delivery or pickup schedule that works for you.
Do you have fresh fish and seafood?+
Yes! We source fresh fish daily from local Molokai fishermen when available. You can typically find ahi (yellowfin tuna), mahi-mahi, and other local catches depending on what's been brought in. Ask at the counter for today's fresh fish selection. It sells out fast, especially on weekends.
Do you accept credit cards and EBT?+
Yes, we accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and EBT (food stamps / SNAP benefits) for eligible grocery purchases. We want to make fresh, healthy food accessible to every Molokai family.
Do you offer a discount for bringing reusable bags?+
Yes! We offer a small discount (typically $0.05 per bag) when you bring your own reusable shopping bags. It's a small gesture toward sustainability, and it adds up over time. We also sell our own branded Aloha Corner Market tote bags near the checkout.
Do you sell poi and traditional Hawaiian foods?+
Yes! We carry fresh poi, kulolo (taro and coconut pudding), raw taro root, and other traditional Hawaiian foods. Poi is available in various sourness levels. We also stock other local staples like Hawaiian sea salt, limu (seaweed), and various local preserves and condiments.
